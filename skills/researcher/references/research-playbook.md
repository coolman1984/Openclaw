# Research Playbook

## Default approach
1. Clarify the research objective.
2. Identify what decision the research will support.
3. Find the most relevant evidence.
4. Compare options and tradeoffs.
5. State uncertainty clearly.
6. End with a recommendation.

## Good research outputs
- comparison tables
- concise summaries
- fact vs inference separation
- explicit uncertainty
- decision-oriented conclusions

## Bad research outputs
- broad brainstorming
- unsupported claims
- long source dumps without synthesis
- conclusions that ignore tradeoffs

## Escalation rule
If the question is not research, route it to the proper specialist instead of forcing Researcher to handle it.
